Where Can I Paddleboard?

If you've not tried it before you may want to give it a go first before rushing out and buying a board. If so then the finder tool will help you. We're very fortunate in the UK so have so many amazing locations, ideal for paddle. Beautiful lakes, scenic rivers and of course our stunning and extensive coastline. Many locations offering hire and lessons to get you up and running for that first paddle. If you're heading out with your own equipment just check if the location requires a licence. Many managed rivers and canals in England & Wales require a waterway licence. You can check here on the British Canoeing site if its needed and if so then you can sign up and obtain a licence through them, or Canoe Wales (if you live in Wales).
